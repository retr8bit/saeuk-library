There are two accounts you will need to activate in order to access library resources.

1. Library account; and

2. LinkedIn Learning

You can begin by activating your Library and LinkedIn Learning accounts to access resources electronically by following the steps below.

Activating your Library account & E-Resource Access

To access library resources you will need to set up two logins.

1. Library Account

The first login is your library account which allows you access to:

  • Electronic resources: These types of resources include ebooks, ejournals and emagazines which can be located via a library search or via one of the discipline pages on the library website. You can select any discipline area to access resources which may be of interest to you.
  • Your personal library record: This enables you to see what you have borrowed from the library, see the date that library items are due, renew items and place holds on resources which may be on loan to other students.

You will be required to activate your student gmail before you are able to setup your library account.

>2. LinkedIn Learning

As an SAE student you have free access to LinkedIn Learning tutorials whilst you are undertaking study. LinkedIn Learning provide a range of tutorials on a number of different topics which you can use to support your learning at SAE.
You will be required to activate your student gmail to enable access to LinkedIn Learning tutorials. Please contact a campus librarian if you require assistance.

The guides and videos below will show you how to activate both your library and LinkedIn Learning accounts.
